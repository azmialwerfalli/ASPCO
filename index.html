Alhokool Aldahabia Petroleum Services Company

APSCO

we provides a wide range of services to the oil and gas industry including drilling, engineering, construction, fabrication, maintenance and repair services. The company has been in operation since 2009 and has become one of the leading service providers in Libya

Operations

Our company is committed to ensuring a continuous supply of oil products and services by exploiting the vast reserves of crude oil that exist in various parts of the Libyan desert. Our team is continuously working on finding new technologically advanced ways to tackle challenges associated with our business industry.

High quality services

Our staff is a team of highly professional people with an average of, 20 years of experience both in administrative and field work and we, continue to employ the best qualified people in the field

Our History

The company has been growing more and more each year. As we grow, we will always keep our safety as our top priority. The safety of our employees and contractors is not only a company mandate but also a personal commitment to each of them.

care for both personal and the environment

We carry out operations in accordance to standards of the environmental agencies and have a high level of respect for both personal safety, health and the environment.

Services

Surface well test.

Our comprehensive range of SWT solutions includes separators, multiphase meters, and other related equipment. We specialize in providing customized solutions to our clients that meet their specific needs and requirements.

Slickline

APSCO is a leading provider of slickline and wireline services to the oil and gas industry. As one of the most experienced companies in this field

Coiltubing jobs A (N2, Acid, Pumping)

APSCO provides a comprehensive suite of coiltubing services including N2, acid and pumping services. It has the expertise and experience to deliver high quality services in an efficient and cost-effective manner

Cementing job

APSCO is a leading organisation in the cementing job industry, providing high-quality services to its clients. It has been providing its services for over 10 years now and has become an established name in the sector.

Wireline job

Wireline job is one of the most important jobs that APSCO provides. It involves running cables down into wells to measure pressure, temperature, fluid levels and other variables in order to determine what kind of production can be expected from a particular well.
